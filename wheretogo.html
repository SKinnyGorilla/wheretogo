Places in Kuwait

Al-Mubarakiyah

Rating: 4.5

Mubarakiya is an old traditional Kuwaiti market, and it is one of the most famous and best places that every tourist should visit in Kuwait. It offers a vibrant and lively atmosphere where visitors can explore various shops, street vendors, and traditional restaurants. The market is known for its authentic Kuwaiti goods, including clothing, accessories, spices, and handicrafts. It is also a great place to experience the rich cultural heritage of Kuwait.
